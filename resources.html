Email Security: Protecting Your Business Starts Here

Email drives modern business—and it's also the #1 entry point for cyberattacks. From phishing to CEO impersonation, criminals target your inbox. We help organizations build a layered defense that protects reputation, employees, and customers.

🔐

Lock the Front Door: Email Authentication

Attackers impersonate trusted brands to scam customers. We prevent this with three standards:

SPF (Sender Policy Framework)

Specifies which servers can send email from your domain.

DKIM (DomainKeys Identified Mail)

Adds a tamper-proof digital signature to every outgoing message.

DMARC (Domain-based Message Authentication, Reporting & Conformance)

Tells receiving servers to reject emails that fail authentication, stopping spoofing cold.

🛡️

Protect Your People: Phishing-Resistant Authentication

Passwords alone won't stop today's sophisticated scams. We implement:

FIDO2 Security Keys

Physical keys that render stolen passwords useless.

Passwordless Passkeys

Biometric logins that simply won't work on fake phishing sites.

🔍

Catch What Gets Through: Advanced Threat Protection

We deploy tools that inspect every message:

Malicious Attachments

Suspicious files are opened in a secure sandbox to neutralize malware.

Dangerous Links

Links are checked in real-time at the moment of click.

Impersonation Defense

Detects CEO fraud and lookalike domains before they cause damage.

As you add new tools and platforms, your security posture evolves. We provide continuous monitoring to ensure settings remain watertight and new gaps don't emerge.
