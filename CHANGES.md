# Straya Co Website - Modern Redesign

## What's New (March 2026 Refresh)

### Visual Design
- **Modern Color Palette**: Blue/orange/green scheme replacing dated corporate blue
- **Better Typography**: Inter & Space Grotesk fonts for readability
- **Improved Spacing**: Better visual hierarchy and breathing room
- **Interactive Elements**: Hover effects, animations, and transitions

### Key Features
1. **Responsive Layout**: Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
2. **Australian-Focused Messaging**: "IT that works for Aussie businesses" - no corporate wank
3. **Interactive Stats Section**: Animated counters for business metrics
4. **Email Protection**: JavaScript-based email obfuscation to prevent spam
5. **Modern Navigation**: Sticky header with hover effects

### Technical Improvements
- **CSS Grid & Flexbox**: Modern layout techniques
- **CSS Variables**: Consistent theming system
- **Smooth Animations**: Fade-in effects on scroll
- **Mobile-First Design**: Optimized for all screen sizes
- **Accessibility**: Semantic HTML and proper contrast ratios

### Content Updates
- **Clear Value Proposition**: Focus on email security, automation, and business setup
- **Australian Context**: Stats and messaging tailored for AU businesses
- **Call-to-Action**: Multiple contact points (email, phone, contact form)
- **Service Details**: Clear explanation of what Straya Co actually does
