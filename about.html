Who We Are

About Straya Co

Straya Co Pty Ltd is a Sydney-based IT consulting company dedicated to helping Australian businesses secure, automate, and grow their IT infrastructure. Our team includes Azure Certified Solution Architects and CISSP-certified cybersecurity experts, delivering enterprise-grade solutions with a focus on reliability, security, and efficiency.

Founded with a mission to bridge the gap between enterprise-level IT expertise and the needs of Australian small and medium businesses, we bring corporate-grade solutions to businesses of all sizes. We understand the unique challenges faced by Australian companies in today's digital landscape and provide tailored solutions that work within local regulations and business environments.

Our Direction

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower businesses across Australia with secure, efficient, and compliant IT solutions tailored to their unique needs. We believe that every Australian business deserves access to enterprise-grade IT expertise without the enterprise price tag.

Our Vision

A world where Australian businesses can operate confidently, knowing their IT systems are protected, automated, and optimised for growth. We envision a digital landscape where Australian innovation is supported by robust, secure, and efficient technology infrastructure.
