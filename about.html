About Straya Co Pty Ltd

Straya Co Pty Ltd is a Sydney-based IT consulting company helping Australian businesses secure, automate, and grow their IT infrastructure. Our team includes Azure Certified Solution Architects and CISSP-certified cybersecurity experts, delivering enterprise-grade solutions without the enterprise price.

Founded with the vision of making professional IT consulting accessible to businesses of all sizes, we combine deep technical expertise with practical business understanding. We specialise in the unique needs of Australian businesses, ensuring compliance with local regulations and best practices.
