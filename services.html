Email Security (Because Getting Hacked Is a Right Pain in the Arse)

The Gist of It
Email's still how business gets done—and unfortunately, it's also how a bunch of flogs try to ruin your day. We help Aussie businesses keep the scammers at bay, make sure your emails actually land where they're meant to, and stop you from being the next "oh no, not again" story at the pub.

What We Actually Do for You

  • DMARC Setup & Monitoring — Stop random idiots pretending to be you and sending dodgy invoices to your customers. It's your domain—keep it that way.
  • SPF & DKIM Configuration — Get your email authentication sorted so you don't end up in the spam folder next to the Nigerian prince offers.
  • Email Deliverability Tune-Ups — If your important emails keep bouncing or vanishing into the void, we'll find out why and fix the bloody thing.
  • Phishing Protection That Actually Works — Catch the suspicious crap before your team accidentally clicks something they really, really shouldn't.

Why Bother?

  • Your brand looks professional, not like it's run from someone's garage
  • Less time wondering "did that email actually send?"
  • Your staff don't get tricked into handing over the keys to the castle
  • You can actually say "yeah we're compliant with cyber standards" without crossing your fingers

Look, Here's the Thing
Security isn't funny when it goes wrong. But sorting it out doesn't have to be a boring slog with a bunch of wanky consultants who talk at you for hours. We know our stuff, we'll get it sorted, and we won't make you feel like an idiot along the way.

Making Your Docs Do the Heavy Lifting

Tired of Manual Data Entry?
Look, there's nothing wrong with a bit of elbow grease, but when your team spends half their week copying and pasting from PDFs or wrestling with spreadsheets, something's gotta give. We help businesses automate the boring stuff so your people can actually do the work you pay them for.

Here's How We Lend a Hand

  • PDF Data Extraction That Doesn't Suck — Got invoices, reports, or forms stuck in PDFs? We'll pull that data out and pop it straight into Excel, databases, or whatever you're using—no retyping required.
  • Excel Automation So You Can Knock Off on Time — Stop manually updating the same spreadsheets every Friday arvo. We'll set up automations that do the heavy lifting while you're having a cuppa.
  • Workflows That Actually Flow — Emails getting lost? Approvals taking forever? We'll connect the dots so your documents move from one person to the next without you having to chase anyone up.

Why You'll Thank Us Later

  • Get hours back in your week—hours you could spend on actual business stuff, or frankly, just leaving on time for once
  • Stop making silly mistakes because someone typed a number in wrong at 4pm on a Friday
  • Everything runs smoother, which means less stress and fewer "does anyone know where the Smith file went?" moments
  • Your smart people can stop doing admin work and start doing the thinking work they're actually good at

The Honest Truth
